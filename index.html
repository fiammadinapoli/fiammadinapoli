Our story

Fiammadi Napoli was born from a love of simple, honest pizza – thin, blistered bases, quality toppings and the unmistakable aroma of woodfired dough.

After years of working in Italian kitchens and Melbourne pizzerias, our team decided to take the oven on the road. Today, we bring the flavours of Naples directly to homes, warehouses, wineries and office car parks across the city.

Every event is prepared with the same care as a neighbourhood pizzeria: slow-proved dough, imported tomatoes, Fior di Latte and a rotating menu of seasonal specials.

From Naples, with fire & flour.
